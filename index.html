Biography

Hi, welcome to my website! My name is Dylan Roberts and I was born and raised in Farmington Maine. From an early age I was interested in history, reading, and loved to play computer games. As time went one, and my interests developed, I focused in on "tech" building my own computer and starting to program in high school. I then made the decision to major in Computer Science at Dalhousie University, something I look back on and think of one of the best choices I've ever made. Living in a foreign (hey Canada is foreign to me) country game me an opportunity to grow as both a person and a programmer, as I close in on finishing my degree, I am looking for an oppertunity to showcase what I've learned, but most of all continue my education while working in the Industy.
